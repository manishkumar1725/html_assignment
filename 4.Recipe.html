HOW TO MAKE MAGGI

    INGRIDENTS REQUIRED:

  • MAGGI:2 PACKETS
  • ONION:1
  • TOMATO:1
  • OIL:2 TSP
  • WATER:1 GLASS SPICES:AS PER YOUR TASTE

    STEPS TO MAKE MAGGI:

  1. BOIL THE MAGGI FIRST.
  2. THEN CUT ONION AND TOMATO.
  3. FRY ONION & TOMATO,ADD MAGGI AND MAGGI MASALA IN IT.
  4. ADD SALT AND OTHER SPICES AS PER YOUR TATE. ADD WATER IN IT AND WAIT FOR 10 MINUTES.
  5. YOUR MAGGI IS READY.
  6. SERVE.....
